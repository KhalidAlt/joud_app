Joud Cleaning is a tool crafted to help manually clean sample datasets. Users can easily refine text by removing irrelevant portions. If a text lacks context, simply delete it and enter '0' before submitting. Follow these steps:

  1. Review the text and identify non-contextual parts for removal.
  2. If the text is fine as is, skip without entering your name.
  3. Remove irrelevant context, whether in part or whole. Enter your name and submit.
